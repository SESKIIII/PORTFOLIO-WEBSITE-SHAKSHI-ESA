CARTZY

Billing made easy!

This project deals with the problem of overcrowding at the billing counters in supermarkets due to which the shopping experience becomes tiring for the users. Encountering a long queue at the billing counter can be a frustrating and unpleasant experience for users, particularly if they feel that their time is being wasted or if they perceive the wait as unnecessary. So we are looking to the user experience of users to have efficient and quick billing.



Product Description

Have come up with a product which is a Scanner and a Barcode Reader that will be on the trolley. After you are done with the shopping, no matter wherever you are, you just have to read the product. It will automatically get added to the cart, and you will be able to generate the bill and make payment. This will make the user experience less tiring and will make it quick!

How Cartzy Works?
1 scanner to unlock the physical trolley once
logged in, will be attached to the Cartzy Trolleys
2 scanner to download app, will be attached on
the walls of the Cartzy Trolley
Scan the QR code on the Cartzy Trolley
Download the Cartzy App
Open the app and login with ID and Password
Click on Scan icon on the app and scan the QR code to unlock the cart
Now use the bar code reader to read the products which you add to cart physically
Once the products are read it will be added to the online cart automatically
Check your cart online and proceed with payment
Once the payment is done, bill will be received in the app itself

User Journey
Method 1: The user can first scan each product while putting them in cart and then use the app to see the items added and generate the soft copy bill.
Method 2: The user can put the products in the web app and generate the soft copy of the bill.
